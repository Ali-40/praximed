# PraxisMed Responsible Outreach Guardrails

**Owner:** Ali Abdeltawab — PraxisMed  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-10  
**Scope:** All B2B outreach activities for PraxisMed sales

---

## Core principles

This is a manual, individual B2B outreach tool.  
It is **not** an automated marketing system and must **never** be used as one.

---

## What is allowed

- Contacting doctors and practices using **publicly listed** phone numbers, email addresses, and websites from Praxisplan.at or the practice's own public site.
- Sending **individually written, personally reviewed** emails one at a time.
- Making **manual phone calls** one at a time.
- Walk-in visits to practice receptions during normal business hours.
- Using AI-generated draft emails **only as a starting point** — always review and adapt before sending.

---

## What is NOT allowed

### No private data
- Do **not** use private phone numbers.
- Do **not** use personal email addresses (only practice/business contact details).
- Do **not** source contact data from hidden, gated, or non-public systems.

### No patient data — ever
- This is a **B2B sales tracker**. It must **never** contain patient names, health data, appointment details, or any other patient information.
- **No PHI (Protected Health Information)** of any kind.
- Production PHI is NO-GO. This tool is for practice contact details only.

### No auto-calling
- Do **not** use auto-dialers, robocalls, or any automated phone calling.
- Do **not** connect this tool to Twilio, Vapi outbound calls, or any other calling API.
- Every call is a **manual, personal phone call** made by Ali.

### No auto-emailing
- Do **not** send emails automatically or in bulk.
- Do **not** connect this tool to SMTP, Gmail API, SendGrid, Mailgun, or any email sending service.
- Every email is **individually composed and manually sent** by Ali.

### No mass spam
- Do **not** send the same message to hundreds of contacts at once.
- Do **not** use any bulk email or mass outreach platform.
- Outreach must remain **personal and individual**.

### No misleading claims
- Do **not** claim PraxisMed is "DSGVO-zertifiziert" or "fully GDPR compliant" without legal review.
  - Use instead: *"Wir legen großen Wert auf Datenschutz — gerne schicken wir Ihnen unsere Datenschutzunterlagen."*
- Do **not** claim PraxisMed auto-confirms appointments. It does not.
- Do **not** claim PraxisMed gives medical advice or diagnoses. It does not.
- Do **not** promise clinical outcomes.
- Do **not** say PraxisMed replaces LATIDO or existing practice software.

### No bypassing consent or rate limits
- Do **not** bypass Praxisplan.at's rate limits or robots.txt.
- Do **not** scrape data that requires login or CAPTCHA bypass.
- Do **not** circumvent terms of service.

---

## Opt-out and Do not contact

- If any clinic or doctor says they do **not** want to be contacted, **immediately** set:
  `Outreach Status = Do not contact`
- Once marked "Do not contact," **never contact that practice again**.
- The Outreach Copilot will automatically exclude all "Do not contact" leads from future plans.
- Respect all opt-out requests immediately and permanently.

---

## Manual review requirement

- **Every email draft** generated by the Outreach Copilot must be reviewed and approved by Ali before sending.
- **Every phone script** is a guide — adapt to the real conversation.
- The Copilot suggests; Ali decides and acts.

---

## Notes column policy

- The Notes column is for professional outreach context only.
- Do **not** write personal comments about doctors or patients.
- Do **not** write medical assessments or opinions.

---

## Legal context

All outreach must comply with:

- Austrian data protection law (DSG 2018)
- EU GDPR (DSGVO)
- Terms of service of praxisplan.at (operated by Ärztekammer Wien)
- Applicable Austrian UWG provisions on B2B commercial outreach

---

## Summary checklist before contacting any lead

- [ ] Contact detail is publicly listed (not private)
- [ ] Lead is NOT marked "Do not contact"
- [ ] Lead is NOT marked "Not interested"
- [ ] Email was individually written and reviewed
- [ ] No diagnosis, medical advice, or PHI claims in message
- [ ] LATIDO objection answer is ready
- [ ] Ready to accept "no" gracefully and mark tracker
