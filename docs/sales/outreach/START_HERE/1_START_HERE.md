## Step by step

**1. Open `2_TODAY_CALL_LIST.xlsx`**
- It shows the top leads for today, sorted by priority.
- Column "Opening Line" = what to say first.
- Column "Status" = update after each call using the dropdown.

**2. Call down the list**
- Start at row 1. Work your way down.
- Use the phone number in the "Phone" column.
- After each call, fill in Status and Call Result.

**3. If they say "send us an email"**
- Open `3_EMAIL_DRAFTS.md`.
- Find that doctor's name.
- Copy the email text. Paste into your email client. Send manually.
- Update their status in the tracker.

**4. Update the master tracker**
- Open `4_MASTER_TRACKER.xlsx` (the full 1,245-lead database).
- Find the lead and update: Outreach Status, Call Result, Notes, Follow-up Date.
- See `5_AFTER_CALL_UPDATE_GUIDE.md` for exact field instructions.
