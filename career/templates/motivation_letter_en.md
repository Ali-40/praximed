## Style notes

- Professional, confident, direct.
- Do not say "I am very passionate" or "I am a quick learner" — show it with evidence.
- Do not explain your residence permit unless the job asks about it.
- Do not say "I hope to" — say "I will" or "I am."
- Strong Erste cover letter style: specific, mature, structured, honest.
